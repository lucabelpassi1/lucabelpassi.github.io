About Me

Hi, I'm Luca. I believe that a pair of glasses is more than just an accessory—it's a piece of identity.

My photography explores the dialogue between the face and the frame. Based in Italy, I blend the raw authenticity of portraiture with the precision of product photography, looking for that specific moment where design meets emotion.

I strip away the unnecessary to let the texture, the gaze, and the style speak for themselves.

Available for assignments worldwide.
