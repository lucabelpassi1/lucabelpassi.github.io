Profile

The Vision

Searching for the silent dialogue between light and character.

I have been photographing since I was fifteen. For the past few years most of that work has been eyewear: frames, faces, and the odd geometry that happens when the two meet.

Shooting campaigns for an optician taught me the thing a generalist never learns — that a frame is a product with proportions, and that the picture fails the moment you cannot see the eyes behind it. The rest is casting, light, and staying out of the way.

I am based in Pistoia, Tuscany. I study computer science in Pisa and built this site myself.

Available for assignments in Tuscany and beyond.
